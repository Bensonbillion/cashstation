Common questions

Everything you need to know.

We recommend booking at least 3 to 4 weeks before your event, especially for Saturday dates between May and October, our busiest season. That said, if your date is available, we've set up with as little as one week's notice. Check availability early to avoid disappointment.

Custom Novelty Notes are printed with your faces, names, or event branding. They're a personalized keepsake guests love. They are not legal tender. Real $1 USD Bills are actual currency guests can purchase from the machine using their card and use to spray. Most couples offer both at their event.

For Custom Novelty Notes, the couple collects 100% of machine sales revenue. After the event, the net revenue from machine sales is transferred directly to you digitally. No bank run the next morning.

For Real $1 USD Bills, full pricing and revenue details are walked through during the quote process. We keep it transparent so there are no surprises on the night.

The machine requires roughly 3ft by 3ft of floor space and standard electrical access (a regular wall outlet). It fits comfortably in any banquet hall, backyard setup, or event venue. We handle delivery, positioning, and setup. Your venue coordinator just needs to know it's coming.

Yes, 100%. Custom novelty notes are legal novelty items that comply fully with Canadian law (they are not currency and cannot be mistaken for any real bill). Dispensing real $1 USD bills is also fully legal across Canada. We've designed both products specifically to operate within Canadian law with no grey areas.

We test and load the machine before every event. In the unlikely event of a technical issue, we are reachable immediately by phone throughout your event. Our service includes setup support and a post-event pickup. We don't just drop off and disappear.
